A trusted Celle address for 18 years – run with personal care and fresh energy.

The most affordable hotel in Celle city centre – just 50 metres from the train station. 1km to the historic downtown.

Our pre-war building has real character: high ceilings, large windows and individually furnished rooms. A nod to Harry Potter adds a playful touch to our historic staircase.

Perfectly located for:

  • Hannover Hbf – just 20 min by train
  • Hannover Messe – just 40 min by train
  • Congress Union Celle – 5 minutes on foot
  • Legal Examination Centre – 5 minutes on foot
  • Green Park (Trift) with children's playground, free outdoor fitness zone with full-body workout stations (upper body, back, core, legs & more) on cushioned safety flooring – 2 minutes on foot

What we're proud of:

  • Unbeatable location – 50 metres from the train station
  • Free WiFi included
  • Smart internet TV (IPTV) – free-to-air channels only. Need help? Just ask at reception.
  • Free on-site parking, subject to availability. Alternatively: train station car park (Parkhaus) nearby.
  • Everything at your doorstep – bakery, supermarket, sushi, burgers and more within steps
  • Group breakfast buffet available for 10+ guests – please enquire in advance
  • 18 years of trust – personally run with genuine care

Good to know before you book:

  • Bathrooms are compact – typical for historic buildings of this era, reflected in our affordable prices
  • Rooms facing Bahnhofstraße: triple-glazed windows installed in 2013 – quiet with window closed. Prefer extra silence? Request a courtyard room.
  • Furnishings are older but functional and clean, mostly with new laminate flooring
  • Paid TV channels are not available
  • No on-site breakfast
